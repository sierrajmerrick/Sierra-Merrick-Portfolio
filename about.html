Who is Sierra?

Hello, my name is Sierra, a Computer Science student at the University of Montana. I am currently pursuing a wide range of studies, including Software Engineering, Web Development, Game Design, Creative Coding, and Cyber Security. My goal is to find creative and unique ways to combine these disciplines to offer immersive user experiences. I have spent the last year piecing together my portfolio of projects through school assignments, volunteer work, and personal creative pursuits.


Aside from school, I have many different hobbies and passions. In my free time, I really enjoy sewing, costume design, repairing retro technology, playing video and board games, coding, listening to and playing music, reading, writing, drawing, and much, much more!
